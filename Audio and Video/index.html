This short audio introduces product design thinking. It explains that product design balances desirability, feasibility, and viability. A product owner collaborates with designers, engineers, and data teams to prioritize features that solve real user problems. Iteration, prototyping, and user testing are central to the design process. Data science supports decisions by providing user insights and experiments. In MERN stacks, rapid prototypes allow testing front-end experiences while back-end services scale product integrations.
